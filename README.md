# 📊 Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

This repository contains my **first Power BI dashboard project**, focused on analyzing **mobile sales data** to generate meaningful customer and business insights.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into an interactive and easy-to-understand dashboard. It provides a clear view of sales performance across different dimensions such as time, location, products, and customer behavior.

---

## 🔍 Key Highlights

- Total Sales: **769M+**  
- Total Quantity Sold: **19K+**  
- Monthly sales trend analysis  
- City-wise sales distribution (map visualization)  
- Sales by mobile brands and models  
- Transactions by payment methods  
- Customer ratings insights  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI**  
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  
- **DAX** (KPIs and Calculations)  

---

## 📈 Insights

- Identified top-performing cities contributing to maximum sales  
- Observed monthly trends and peak sales periods  
- Analyzed popular mobile brands and models  
- Understood customer preferences through ratings  
- Identified commonly used payment methods
